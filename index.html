You want to be a millionaire or not? is a quiz game based on the popular game show, Who wants to be a Millionaire? but with a singaporean twist!

  • Contestants have to correctly answer a series of multiple-choice questions in order to advance to the next level / question.
  • There are 10 questions in total and each question is worth a specific amount of money and no time limit is placed on contestants to come up with an answer
  • At every level, players can choose from 4 different categories and answer the question
  • If they answer correctly, they will earn a certain amount of money in their bank and can choose tomove on to the next question, or withdraw all the money and quit
  • However, if they answer the question wrongly, they will lose all of the money they earned and go home with nothing!
